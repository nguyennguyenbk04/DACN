\section{Goals}
The purpose of \textbf{Quizum} is to create an intelligent web-based platform that transforms long educational video lectures into structured, digestible, and interactive learning materials. By integrating state-of-the-art speech recognition and natural language processing (NLP) models, the system automatically generates accurate transcripts, concise summaries, and self-assessment quizzes. \textbf{Quizum} aims to improve study efficiency, enhance learner engagement, and support instructors in delivering more effective and accessible educational experiences.
