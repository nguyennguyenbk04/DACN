\section{Motivation}
In an era where digital learning has become essential, students and self-learners frequently face the challenge of managing and understanding large volumes of unstructured information. Lecture recordings, research papers, notes, and online resources often require significant time to process, summarize, and convert into useful study materials. Traditional learning tools provide limited assistance, leaving learners to manually extract key ideas, organize concepts, and create revision materials.\\

Quizum is motivated by the need to address these challenges through a modern, intelligent, and automated learning-support platform. By integrating technologies such as transcription, natural language processing, mind-map generation, and AI-powered interaction, Quizum aims to help learners transform raw content into structured, digestible knowledge. Instead of passively consuming information, users can actively explore summaries, generate quizzes, ask questions via a chatbot, and visualize connections between concepts.\\

As personalized and self-paced learning becomes increasingly important, there is a growing demand for platforms that adapt to individual needs and reduce manual effort in study preparation. Quizum is designed to meet this need by providing tools that enhance comprehension, automate content organization, and support deeper understanding.\\

Ultimately, the motivation behind Quizum is to empower learners with an interactive, AI-assisted environment that makes learning more efficient, engaging, and accessible—helping users focus on understanding rather than spending time reconstructing information.\\
