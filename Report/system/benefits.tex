\section{Benefits of the system}
Quizum brings significant benefits to both learners and educators by making education more efficient, accessible, and engaging. For students, it saves valuable time by generating concise summaries and quizzes that allow them to grasp key concepts quickly, review lessons effectively, and test their understanding without rewatching lengthy lectures. This not only improves retention but also supports personalized learning, as students can focus on the areas where they struggle most. Educators benefit from enhanced teaching effectiveness, as the platform provides tools to track how students interact with content and highlights knowledge gaps that may require further clarification. Institutions also gain value through improved student engagement and performance, which directly supports their academic goals while showcasing innovation in digital learning. Overall, the system transforms passive video consumption into an interactive and supportive learning experience that benefits all stakeholders involved.
