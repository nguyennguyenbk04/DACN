\section {AI and NLP}
AI is at the heart of \textit{Quizum}’s functionality. The system integrates advanced NLP models to automatically extract meaningful content from video lectures and transform it into structured summaries and quiz questions:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Whisper is employed for speech-to-text transcription. This model is capable of producing highly accurate transcriptions even in the presence of background noise or varying speaker accents, making it suitable for real-world classroom recordings and online lectures.
    \item Google's PEGASUS (Pre-training with Extracted Gap-sentences for Abstractive SUmmarization) model for automatic text summarization
    \item google/flan-t5-base model is chosen for MCQ generation because it is "instruction-tuned," making it an ideal starting point for a specific instruction-based task 
\end{itemize}
